Ingredients

  • 80% lean/20% fat ratio ground chuck
  • Crushed crackers or Panko bread crumbs
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Sesame seed bun

Steps

  1. Add ground beef, crushed crackers and Worcestershire sauce to a large mixing bowl
  2. Use your hands to thoroughly combine the mixture until it is smooth
  3. Press the meat down in the bowl to a 1/2 inch thick disk.
