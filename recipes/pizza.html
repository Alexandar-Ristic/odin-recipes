Pepperoni Pizza!

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a classic fast-food favorite adored by children and adults alike. With the simple but timeless combination of fresh mozzarella cheese, slightly greasy pepperoni and a golden crust!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 can CONTADINA Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon crushed, dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 envelops FLEISCHMANN'S Pizza Crust Yeast
  • 1 package HORMEL Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425 Degrees F (220 degrees C)
  2. Grease two 12-inch pizza pans.
  3. Whisk together water, tomato paste and garlic powder
  4. Combine 2 cups flour, yeast and sugar in a large bowl
  5. Top dough with sauce, cheese and pepperoni
