Spicy Ramen

Description

Japanese noodle dish. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles (or 中華麺, chūkamen) served in a broth; common flavors are soy sauce and miso, with typical toppings including sliced pork (chāshū), nori (dried seaweed), menma (bamboo shoots), and scallions. (Wikipedia)

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Wheat noodles
  • Sliced, cooked pork
  • Dried seaweed
